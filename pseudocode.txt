Connect 4 is a two-player game with red and yellow pieces.
The grid is 7x6 and will need to be printed after each turn.
Red player will start first, and then red and yellow players take turns starting first in consecutive games.
Red player picks a row and drops a piece in that slot.
Play alternates until a player gets 4 pieces in a row horizontally, vertically or diagonally.
Players can choose to play again or stop playing at the end of a match.
